Hey there, fellow gamer!

Many years ago, I fell in love with Diablo 2 and its mods. I also love to program, and from the union of these two passions, this project was born: a Holy Grail tracker for PD2.

If you like this tool or find it useful, you can contribute your ideas or report any bugs in my project open_in_new.

This is the administration panel for the tool. It only has three buttons:

  • Download: This creates a backup copy on your device.
  • Reset: This deletes all information. Be careful!
  • Upload: This recovers a backup copy from your device.

This way, you can keep track of multiple Holy Grails for each season and character, for example.
